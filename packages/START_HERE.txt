Traditional AMM Problem:
  Market price changes â†’ Arbitrageurs extract value â†’ LPs lose money
  This is called "divergence loss" or "impermanent loss"

Your Pseudo-Arbitrage Solution:
  Market price changes â†’ Curve transforms automatically â†’ LPs keep value
  No arbitrage opportunity = No loss for LPs!

Based on:
  Engel & Herlihy paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.00667
  Section 6.1 - Pseudo-Arbitrage Strategy
